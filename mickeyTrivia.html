15 FUN FACTS ABOUT MICKEY MOUSE

1. Mickey became an instant hit with the release of ‘Steamboat Willie’, the first ever cartoon with synchronised sound.

2. Walt originally wanted to call Mickey ‘Mortimer Mouse’.

3. In ‘Opry House’, a video short, Mickey started wearing white gloves./p>

4. Mickey became the first cartoon character to have a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, in honour of his 50th anniversary.

5. Walt Disney accepted a deal with a stationery company for the right to imprint Mickey Mouse on school writing tablets.

6. One of the most iconic Mickey Mouse products of all time is the Mickey Mouse wrist watch.

7. The creation of Mickey Mouse earned Walt Disney an honorary Academy Award.

8. Mickey was given his own entry in the Encyclopedia Britannica.

9. Mickey appeared in colour for the first time in ‘The Band Concert’.

10. Mickey appeared in black and white for the last time in ‘Mickey’s Kangaroo’.

11. Mickey Mouse’s feature film debut in ‘Fantasia’ as the Sorcerer’s Apprentice was one of his most famous roles ever.

12. Mickey made his debut on television in The Mickey Mouse Club television show.

13. Mickey and Minnie Mouse have never been married on screen.

14. In designing and constructing a Disney theme park or adding the final touches to an attraction, Disney Imagineers subtly “hide” Mickey Mouse silhouettes in plain sight. It later became a tradition known as ‘Hidden Mickeys’.

15. Mickey’s favourite sayings are “Oh Boy!”, “Gosh!”, “That sure is swell”, “Aw, gee…”, “See ya soon!”
